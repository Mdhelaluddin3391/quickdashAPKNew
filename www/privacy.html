Privacy Policy

At QuickDash, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery addresses.
  • Location Data: Precise or approximate location to show serviceable restaurants and deliver your orders accurately.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our app, including device info and IP address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to process your orders, facilitate deliveries, communicate with you regarding your account, and improve our platform's services.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share necessary information (like name, phone number, and address) with vendors (restaurants/stores) and our delivery partners strictly for the purpose of fulfilling your order.

4. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can manage these settings directly within your profile in the app.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team via the Support section in the app.
